Movies

Fase one

Iron Man

Tony Stark is a brilliant weapons inventor who is kidnapped abroad. His captors are terrorists who force him to build a destructive machine, but Tony will build an armor to be able to face them and escape.

The Hulk

Bruce Banner travels the world in search of an antidote to get rid of his alter ego. He will also have to face Emil, a new enemy, which will turn New York into the scene of the last battle between the two most powerful creatures.

Iron Man 2

With the world now aware that he is Iron Man, millionaire inventor Tony Stark must forge new alliances and face a powerful new enemy.

thor

After unleashing an ancient war, the greedy warrior Thor is banished to Earth by his father to live among men and discover the true meaning of humility. There, without his powers, Thor must face the darkest forces that his greatest enemy will send from Asgard.

Captain america

After three months of undergoing a physical and tactical training program, Steve Rogers is assigned his first mission as Captain America. Armed with an indestructible shield, he will wage war against Evil as the leader of the Avengers.

avengers

The director of the SHIELD Agency decides to recruit a team to save the world from almost certain disaster when an unexpected enemy emerges as a major threat to global security.
