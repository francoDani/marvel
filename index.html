Universe

Movies

phase one

The first phase introduces the universe in which the films are set, introducing the central characters: Anthony Stark, Steven Rogers, Thor, Bruce Banner, Natalia Romanoff, Clinton Barton, and Nicholas Fury, as well as organizations such as S.H.I.E.L.D. and HYDRA. Many of the Phase One movies focus on the Tesseract, an alien artifact that was later established as one of the six Infinity Stones. The last movie, The Avengers, involves the villains Loki and Thanos teaming up to steal the Tesseract and conquer Earth, leading to the formation of the Avengers.

Iron Man

Tony Stark is a brilliant weapons inventor who is kidnapped abroad. His captors are terrorists who force him to build a destructive machine, but Tony will build an armor to be able to face them and escape.

The Hulk

Bruce Banner travels the world in search of an antidote to get rid of his alter ego. He will also have to face Emil, a new enemy, which will turn New York into the scene of the last battle between the two most powerful creatures.

Iron Man 2

With the world now aware that he is Iron Man, millionaire inventor Tony Stark must forge new alliances and face a powerful new enemy.

thor

After unleashing an ancient war, the greedy warrior Thor is banished to Earth by his father to live among men and discover the true meaning of humility. There, without his powers, Thor must face the darkest forces that his greatest enemy will send from Asgard.

Captain america

After three months of undergoing a physical and tactical training program, Steve Rogers is assigned his first mission as Captain America. Armed with an indestructible shield, he will wage war against Evil as the leader of the Avengers.

avengers

The director of the SHIELD Agency decides to recruit a team to save the world from almost certain disaster when an unexpected enemy emerges as a major threat to global security.

phase two

The first phase introduces the universe in which the films are set, introducing the central characters: Anthony Stark, Steven Rogers, Thor, Bruce Banner, Natalia Romanoff, Clinton Barton, and Nicholas Fury, as well as organizations such as S.H.I.E.L.D. and HYDRA. Many of the Phase One movies focus on the Tesseract, an alien artifact that was later established as one of the six Infinity Stones. The last movie, The Avengers, involves the villains Loki and Thanos teaming up to steal the Tesseract and conquer Earth, leading to the formation of the Avengers.

Iron Man 3

Brash and brilliant Tony Stark, having seen his entire personal universe destroyed, must find and confront an enemy whose power knows no bounds. This journey will test his mettle over and over again, forcing him to trust his wits.

Thor: the dark world

Malekith, an enemy older than the universe, returns to Earth to fulfill his destructive plan. Thor must face a rival that not even Odin seems to be able to stop and, in desperation, he frees his brother Loki to help him.

Captain America

the winter soldier

After the devastating events in New York, the superhero lives quietly in Washington D.C., trying to adapt to the modern world. However, when a colleague of Rogers is threatened, Rogers finds himself embroiled in a web of intrigue.

guardians of the galaxy

Daredevil adventurer Peter Quill, after stealing a mysterious sphere coveted by Ronan, a powerful villain whose ambitions threaten the entire universe, becomes the most coveted prey for all the bounty hunters in the galaxy.

Avengers

Age of ultron

Tony Stark wants to launch a new peace program, but something goes wrong and he ends up creating Ultron, a robot that wants to destroy humanity. Thor, Hulk and the rest of the Avengers must join forces once again to fight the robot.

Ant man

Doctor Hank Pym announces that he is leaving S.H.I.E.L.D. after discovering that they have tried to appropriate his Ant-Man's technology, which Pym considers highly dangerous. Several years later, Scott Lang is released from prison after serving three years for robbery.
